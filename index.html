As the rise of new ways to gamble on football continues, the amount of money bet becomes staggering. During the 2015 season alon, an estimated $1.34 billion was bet the traditional way, $1.8 was bet in fantasy leagues, and an incredible $93 billion was bet illegally. To be able to consistently make money on these bets, gamblers must spend countless hours studying team statistics and consider an immense amount of factors leading up to the game.
